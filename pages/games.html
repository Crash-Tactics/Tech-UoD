What is Games Programming?

"Games Programming" refers to the development of computer games, including the creation of game mechanics, graphics, audio, and overall functionality. Game programmers use programming languages and tools to bring game designs to life, implementing features like player controls, artificial intelligence, physics simulations, and networking capabilities. They work closely with game designers, artists, and other developers to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences across various platforms such as consoles, PCs, mobile devices, and virtual reality systems.
