What is Computer Science?

Computer Science offers a deep dive into the essentials of computational theory. This field is not just about coding, it’s about solving complex problems developing new ways to interact with technology and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.

What you'll learn

Computer science at Derby offers students a course filled with knowledge on the theory aspect of computer science, with a strong emphasis on practical projects using cutting-edge software in the professional laboratories on campus.

Students can expect to learn about developing high-quality software in our specialist facility labs, such as the XR (Cross-reality) lab whereby students can develop games and visualise various software projects, the CAVE lab which allows students to project video and sound in a 270 degree Virtual Reality environment, and the Makers space where students can work on their personal projects and make prototypes.

What's next?

After completing this course, a wide range of career opportunities will become available including:

  • Software engineer
  • Data analyst
  • Web developer
  • Many other opportunities as a degree in computer science can open up pathways to more careers in thetechnology field.

Employers love experience! Thankfully, UoD offers students the chance to explore many graduate programmes, as well as providing their own placement year options!

Why start now?

Computer science as a whole is a great opportunity for students to consider due to the variety of career options and the extensive job market. Computer scientists are needed in most industries as humans steer more toward a world with technological aid. Computer systems and software are constantly evolving which will require specialists far into the future.
