What is Computer Games Programming?

Games programming is what makes games engaging, containing everything from logic-driven code and the physics of game mechanics to the artistry of graphics and how optimisation can make a user’s experience second to none.

What you'll learn

Games Design at Derby offers an exciting learning experience filled with engaging theory and fun practical projects. From your first year you will be tasked with the creation of computer games from the very beginning. Students will gain experience in writing code, gaining experience in professional game engines such as unity and unreal engine, and learn to design characters and worlds within these environments. .

What's next?

After completing this course, a wide range of career opportunities will become available including:

  • 3D animator
  • Application developer
  • Games creative designer
  • Many other opportunities with a degree in computer games programming can open up pathways to more careers in thetechnology field.

Employers love experience! Thankfully, UoD offers students the chance to explore many graduate programmes, as well as providing their own placement year options!

Why start now?

Computer games have become a primary source of entertainment within many lives. More than ever, smaller startup companies have the opportunity to create intriguing concepts for games due to the powerful tools at our disposal. The market share for non-Triple-A titles is increasing meaning that aspiring game developers don’t have to join a large company to be successful.
