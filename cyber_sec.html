What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security protects the digital foundation and sensitive data prioritising user’s safety online. Creating a secure environment from an ever-evolving array of cyber threats.

What you'll learn

Choosing cyber-security as an undergraduate degree offers not just a way into a field that plays a major part in today’s digital world, but also an abundance of exciting opportunities.

Students can expect to engage in hands-on learning through simulated cyber attacks, ethical hacking exercises, as well as internships with leading tech firms such as IBM.

Derby’s ability to provide students with real world training provides them with a unique blend of technical skills, ethical understanding, and analytical capabilities.

What's next?

After completing this course, a wide range of career opportunities will become available including:

  • Software engineer
  • Digital Forensic Investigator
  • Security analyst
  • Many other opportunities with a degree in cyber security can open up pathways to more careers in thetechnology field.

Employers love experience! Thankfully, UoD offers students the chance to explore many graduate programmes, as well as providing their own placement year options!

Why start now?

Cyber Security is an integral part of our daily lives due to our rapidly increasing reliance on technology. Every device that we use has the potential to be compromised by hackers. Taking this into account, more than ever cyber security specialists are needed to help mitigate these evolving threats. That being said Cyber security jobs will always be in demand as technology evolves and therefore, threats evolve alongside it.
